Travel & Directions to Dhanbad

We can't wait to celebrate with you! To help you plan your journey, here are the best ways to reach Dhanbad:

By Air

Dhanbad does not have its own airport. If you are flying in, we recommend these options:

  • Kazi Nazrul Islam Airport (DGP), Durgapur: Closest airport (approx. 1.5‚Äì2 hours drive). Best for domestic flights from major cities.
  • Birsa Munda Airport (IXR), Ranchi: About 3‚Äì4 hours drive. Often more frequent flight options.
  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU), Kolkata: About 4.5‚Äì5 hours drive. Best for international guests or long-distance domestic flights.
By Train

Dhanbad Junction is a major rail hub and is often the most convenient way to reach us.

  • Key trains: Howrah-New Delhi Rajdhani, Shatabdi, and Black Diamond Express all stop here.
  • The station is centrally located, making it easy to grab a quick taxi or auto-rickshaw to your hotel.
By Bus
  • Regular AC/Volvo bus services run from Kolkata, Ranchi, Jamshedpur, and Patna.
  • The main bus stand is located at Bartand, near the city center.
By Cab / Driving
  • Dhanbad is located on National Highway 19 (Grand Trunk Road).
  • Approximate travel times: From Ranchi: 3.5 hours. From Asansol: 1.5 hours. From Kolkata: 5 hours.
